General Questions

Will I receive a certificate of completion or professional certification upon finishing a course?

+

Yes! Upon successful completion of all course modules, projects, and final assessments, you will receive an official BUILD SCHOOL Certificate of Completion. This is a great credential to share on your resume and LinkedIn profile to validate your newly acquired skills.

What equipment or software will I need to participate in the online courses?

+

All you need is a reliable internet connection, a laptop or desktop computer, and a modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge). Any specialized software required (like a code editor or data analysis tools) is either free/open-source or provided to you via a temporary educational license.

What kind of support is available if I get stuck on a difficult concept or project?

+

You will never be learning alone! Our support system includes dedicated TAs (Teaching Assistants) available during scheduled "office hours," an active private student community forum for peer-to-peer help, and direct mentorship from your expert instructor during live sessions.

+

We believe in lifelong learning. All enrolled students receive lifetime access to the video lectures, downloadable resources, project files, and any future updates to the curriculum for the course they completed. This ensures you can always refresh your knowledge.
